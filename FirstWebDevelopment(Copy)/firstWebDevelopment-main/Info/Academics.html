A. Physical Development

To develop muscular control of the small and big muscles, health, the habit of hygiene, and coordination movement through rhythmic and gymnastic activity and games, indoor and outdoor free play.



B. Intellectual Development

i. Achieve refinement of the senses as receptors for developmental experiences and skill in using one’s fingers ii. Awaken the child’s cognitive power and develop the ability to think, observe, classify, plan, communicate and preserve by him/herself rather than accumulate and memorize knowledge. iii. Form basic concepts through first-hand experience in self-chosen, guided, creative and constructive play activities. iv. To develop self-confidence, initiative and emotional security through the experience of success and acceptance; to develop a happy attitude towards life as a necessary pre-requisite to successful living and learning. v. Develop creative self-expression and a sense for beauty, performing arts through a rich program of art activity, musical experiences and appreciation of the beauty of nature. vi. Develop in the child the desire to live as a true Lasallian and a child of God by actual application of the Lasallian values and virtues, kindness, truth and honesty. Appreciation of work and the desire to do good, respect the rights of others, to share and to follow accepted rules of democratic living.


FURTHER CONTEXT

DMC College Foundation, located in Dipolog City, Philippines, has a rich history dating back to 1974 when it was founded as a 50-bed general hospital by 15 Dipolog citizens, including then Dipolog Archbishop Msgr. Felix Sanchez Zafra and 15 medical practitioners. The college started offering midwifery courses in 1977 and later expanded to offer nursing, physical therapy, radiologic technology, and medical technology programs. In 1989, a new management team took over, and the college was renamed Dipolog Medical Center College of Health Sciences in 1991. The college continued to grow and expand its programs, including the addition of secondary education, computer science, criminology, and business courses.


MISSION

We set high standards in the performance of our students, faculty and co-academic personnel to achieve optimum integral human development by continuously enriching our academic, co-academic and leadership programs with technology-based instruction, team research and the promotion of athletics, culture and arts grounded on nationalism.

VISION

DMC College Foundation is a leading educational institution, pursuing excellence in its socially oriented and progressive academic programs and services forming globally competent graduates who are active partners in nation-building.
