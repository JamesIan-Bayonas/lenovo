About Us

DM College is a private higher education institution located in the heart of Manila. We offer a wide range of programs in the fields of business, education, and technology.

Our mission is to provide quality education that prepares students for successful careers and responsible citizenship.

Our History

DM College was founded in 1969 by a group of dedicated educators who envisioned a institution that would provide quality education to students from all walks of life.

Over the years, we have grown and expanded our programs to meet the changing needs of our students and the community.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leading institution of higher learning that produces graduates who are competent, compassionate, and committed to making a positive impact in society.

We strive to create a learning environment that is conducive to academic excellence, personal growth, and social responsibility.

Our Values

We value academic excellence, integrity, respect, and social responsibility.

We believe in the importance of fostering a culture of excellence, innovation, and collaboration.
