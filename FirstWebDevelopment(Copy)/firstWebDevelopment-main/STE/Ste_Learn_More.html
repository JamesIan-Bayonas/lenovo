Vision and Mission

The School of Teacher Education envisions itself as a premier institution in teacher education, producing highly competent, compassionate, and committed teachers who will make a difference in the lives of their students and in the community.

The mission of the School of Teacher Education is to provide quality education that is responsive to the needs of the times, and to produce teachers who are equipped with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary to meet the challenges of the 21st century.

Academic Programs

The School of Teacher Education offers the following academic programs:

  • Bachelor of Elementary Education (BEEd)
  • Bachelor of Secondary Education (BSEd) major in English, Filipino, Mathematics, Science, and Social Studies
  • Bachelor of Special Needs Education (BSNEd)
  • Master of Arts in Education (MAEd) major in Educational Management, Curriculum and Instruction, and Guidance and Counseling
  • Doctor of Philosophy in Education (PhD Ed) major in Educational Management and Curriculum and Instruction



Faculty and Staff

The School of Teacher Education is composed of highly qualified and experienced faculty and staff who are committed to providing quality education to its students.

The faculty members are experts in their respective fields and have undergone rigorous training and development to ensure that they are updated with the latest trends and best practices in education.

Research and Extension

The School of Teacher Education is committed to conducting research and extension activities that are relevant to the needs of the community and the education sector.

The school has established partnerships with various organizations and institutions to promote collaborative research and extension activities.



Admission Requirements

The admission requirements for the School of Teacher Education are as follows:

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Passing grade in the entrance examination
  • Good moral character
  • Passing grade in the interview

Scholarship and Financial Assistance

The School of Teacher Education offers various scholarship and financial assistance programs to its students.

These programs are designed to help students who are in need of financial assistance to pursue their studies.
