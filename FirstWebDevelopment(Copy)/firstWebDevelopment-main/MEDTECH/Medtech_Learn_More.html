Vision and Mission

The Allied Medical Sciences - MedTech program envisions itself as a premier institution in medical technology education, producing highly competent, compassionate, and committed medical technologists who will make a difference in the lives of their patients and in the community.

The mission of the Allied Medical Sciences - MedTech program is to provide quality education that is responsive to the needs of the times, and to produce medical technologists who are equipped with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary to meet the challenges of the 21st century.

Academic Programs

The Allied Medical Sciences - MedTech program offers the following academic programs:

  • Bachelor of Science in Medical Technology (BSMT)
  • Master of Science in Medical Technology (MSMT)



Faculty and Staff

The Allied Medical Sciences - MedTech program is composed of highly qualified and experienced faculty and staff who are committed to providing quality education to its students.

The faculty members are experts in their respective fields and have undergone rigorous training and development to ensure that they are updated with the latest trends and best practices in medical technology.

Research and Extension

The Allied Medical Sciences - MedTech program is committed to conducting research and extension activities that are relevant to the needs of the community and the healthcare sector.

The program has established partnerships with various organizations and institutions to promote collaborative research and extension activities.



Admission Requirements

The admission requirements for the Allied Medical Sciences - MedTech program are as follows:

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Passing grade in the entrance examination
  • Good moral character
  • Passing grade in the interview

Scholarship and Financial Assistance

The Allied Medical Sciences - MedTech program offers various scholarship and financial assistance programs to its students.

These programs are designed to help students who are in need of financial assistance to pursue their studies.
